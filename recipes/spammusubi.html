Delicious Spam Musubi recipe

Try this tasty Hawaiian staple with delicious pan-seared Spam atop a bed of lightly seasoned rice, wrapped in seaweed

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can of Spam
  • 2 cups of rice, dry
  • 1 package of seaweed wraps
  • Furikake seasoning to taste

Directions:

  1. Rinse the rice through water until the runoff is clear. May take two to three rinses.
  2. Put rice and 3 cups water in a pot, bring to a boil
  3. Once the rice is brought to a boil, drop the temperature to low and cover with a lid
  4. Simmer rice until water has absorbed completely
  5. Bring a pan to medium heat on the stove
  6. Cut the spam into eight slices, sear on both sides until crispy
  7. Drizzle your favorite teriyaki sauce onto the spam
  8. Using a musubi press, form rice into desired thickness. 1/2 inch minimum.
  9. Sprinkle Furikake seasoning onto beds of rice, top with spam
  10. Cut seaweed into strips to wrap around the middle of the musubi. Use preference to get desired width
